Microbiome signature of Parkinsonšs disease in healthy and genetically at-risk individuals

This codebook contains the definition of the clinical variables used in the manuscript "Microbiome signature of Parkinsonšs disease in healthy and genetically at-risk individualsė and presented in the UCL Research Data Repository at the link: https://doi.org/10.5522/04/30710741.

List of clinical variables and their description

clinical_ID
Unique, anonymous ID related to the participant

MGP_ID_fecal_baseline
Unique, anonymous ID related to the participant's faecal sample

cohort
Individual belonging cohort [ITA = Italy; GBR: Great Britain (UK)] 

age
Age [years]

sex
Sex [Female/Male]

partner_updated
Participation with a partner in the study [yes/no]

blood_related_updated
Participation with a family member in the study [yes/no]

bmi
Body Mass Index [kg/m2]

group
Belonging group, x4 levels [GBA-NMC, GBA1 non manifesting carrier; GBA-PD, GBA1 Parkinsonšs disease; HC, healthy control; sPD, sporadic/idiopathic Parkinsonšs disease]

three_group
Belonging group, x3 levels, gathering GBA-PD and sPD together [GBA-NMC, GBA1 non manifesting carrier; HC, healthy control; PD, Parkinsonšs disease]

ed_yrs
Years of education [yrs]

fhx
Positive family history for Parkinsonšs disease, x3 levels [no, negative family history; yes_first, first-degree relative member; yes_second, second-degree relative member]

fhx2
Positive family history for Parkinsonšs disease, x2 levels, gathering first- and second-degree relative member together [no, negative family history; yes, positive family history, either first- or second-degree relative member]

pd_criteria
PD diagnostic criteria according to the UK Biobank criteria or the 2015 MDS Parkinson's disease Clinical Criteria [yes/no]

tbi
Traumatic brain injury [yes/no]

appendectomy
Appendectomy [yes/no]

appendectomy_ageatsurgery
Appendectomy age at surgery

appendectomy_pd_yearsdiff
Years of difference between appendectomy and Parkinsonšs disease onset [years]

coffee
Coffee consumption [yes/no]

coffee_amount				
Amount of coffee consumption [less_than_one_cup, less than 1 cup; one_small_cup, 1 small cup; one_medium_cup, 1 medium cup; one_big_cup, 1 big cup; more_one_big_cup, more than 1 big cup]

smoking
Smoking habit [yes/no]

smoking_current_duration
Duration of smoking for current smokers [years]

smoking_ex_duration
Duration of smoking for ex-smokers [years]

smoking_packs
Number of smoked packs of cigarettes

updrs_i_total
MDS-UPDRS part I score

updrs_ii_total
MDS-UPDRS part II score

hoehn_yahr
H & Y stage

updrs_iii_total
MDS-UPDRS part III score

updrs_iv_total
MDS-UPDRS part IV score

updrs_total
MDS-UPDRS total score

moca_raw_total
MOCA raw score (without adjustment for years of education)

education_n_years
Number of years in education collected during MOCA questionnaire 

moca_total
MOCA score adjusted for years of education (to adjust MOCA score, the variable "ed_yrs" was used)

scopa_total
SCOPA-AUT total score

scopa_gastrointestinal
SCOPA Gastrointestinal score

scopa_urinary
SCOPA Urinary score

scopa_cardiovascular
SCOPA Cardiovascular score

scopa_thermoregulatory
SCOPA Thermoregulatory score

scopa_pupillomotor
SCOPA Pupillomotor score

scopa_sexual
SCOPA Sexual score

hads_depression_total
HADS depression score

hads_anxiety_total
HADS anxiety score

rbdsq_total
RBDSQ (raw score)

bdi_total
Beck Depression Inventory score

upsit_total
UPSIT score

wcs_total
Wexner Constipation Scoring System score

seborr_dermatitis
Seborrheic dermatitis [yes/no]

seborr_dermatitis_ageonset
Age at onset of seborrheic dermatitis [years]

seborr_dermatitis_pd_datediff
Years of difference between seborrheic dermatitis onset and Parkinsonšs disease onset [years]

pd_dbs	
Deep brain stimulation [yes/no]

pd_dbs_years
Time between deep brain stimulation surgery and clinical evaluation [years]

pd_dbs_target
Deep brain stimulation target

pd_lcig
Levodopa carbidopa intestinal gel [yes/no]

pd_apomorphine
Apomorphine pump [yes/no]

pd_onset_calculated
Parkinsonšs disease age of onset [years]

pd
Parkinsonšs disease diagnosis [yes/no]

pd_dur
Parkinsonšs disease duration [years]

pd_drug_naive
Drug naĢve Parkinson's disease participant [yes/no]

ledd_total
Levodopa equivalent daily dose [mgs]

diet
Diet type

fruit_portion
Portions of fruit eaten per day [None; 1 portion; 2 portions; 3 portions; 4 portions; 5 portions; More than 5 portions]

vegetables_portion
Portions of vegetables eaten per day [None; 1 portion; 2 portions; 3 portions; 4 portions; 5 portions; More than 5 portions]

milk
Milk consumption [Whole/full-fat milk; Semi-skimmed milk; Skimmed milk; Rarely/never use milk; Others]

alcohol_intake
Alcohol intake [I rarely/never drink alcohol; Less than 14 units; Between 14 and 21 units; More than 21 units]

smoking_pd_biome
Smoking status (information collected through self-administered survey) [A current smoker; An ex-smoker; I have never smoked more than 100 cigarettes]

physical_activity
Physical activity [No activity; Less that 1 hour/week; 1-2 hours/week; 2-4 hours/week; More that 4 hours/week]

physical_activity_threshold
Physical activity defined using threshold of 150 minutes of moderate-intensity physical activity/week recommended for adults in the UK [active if 2-4 hours/week OR More that 4 hours/week; not active if No activity OR Less that 1 hour/week OR 1-2 hours/week]

out_breath_stairs
Out of breath when walking 3 levels of stairs [yes/no]

Fruit
Fruit intake calculated through Dietary quality score calculator

Vegetables
Vegetables intake calculated through Dietary quality score calculator

Oily_Fish
Oily fish intake calculated through Dietary quality score calculator

Fat
Fat intake calculated through Dietary quality score calculator

NMES
Non-milk extrinsic sugars intake calculated through Dietary quality score calculator

DQS_score
Dietary quality score 

hads_depression_range
Range of depression according to HADS Depression Scale

hads_anxiety_range
Range of anxiety according to HADS Anxiety Scale

bdi_range
Range of depression according to BDI Scale

rbd_affected_diff
RBD affected according to RBDSQ score

upsit_range
Range of olfactory dysfunction according to UPSIT Scale

wcs_threshold
Constipation according to WCS threshold

moca_range
Cognition dysfunction according to MOCA score (abnormal/cognitive impairment defined as a MOCA score <26/30)

subthr_park
Subthreshold parkinsonism calculated according to MDS prodromal criteria  

subthr_park_threshold
Subthreshold parkinsonism defined is score > 6 according to MDS prodromal criteria  

sleepiness
Sleepiness score (Question 1.8 MDS-UPDRS part I)

sleepiness_prodromal_criteria
Sleepiness defined is score > or = 3

urinary_dysfunction
Urinary dysfunction (Question 1.10 MDS-UPDRS part I)

urinary_dysfunction_prodromal_criteria
Urinary dysfunction defined is score > or = 2

erectyle_dysfunction
Erectile dysfunction (Question 22 SCOPA-AUT)

erectyle_dysfunction_prodromal_criteria
Erectile dysfunction defined is score > or = 3

physical_activity_threshold_prodromal_criteria
Physical inactivity defined if less than 1 hour a week

t2_diabetes
Presence of diabete mellitus type 2 according to medical history

prior_probability
Prior probability calculated according to MDS prodromal criteria  

risk_markers
Risk markers calculated according to MDS prodromal criteria  

prodromal_markers
Prodromal markers calculated according to MDS prodromal criteria  

clinical_nonmotor_markers
Clinical non-motor markers calculated according to MDS prodromal criteria  

clinical_motor_markers
Clinical motor markers calculated according to MDS prodromal criteria  

lr_threshold
Total Likelihood Ratio Threshold Required for Probable Prodromal PD calculated according to MDS prodromal criteria  

estimated_lr
Total Estimated Likelihood Ratio calculated according to MDS prodromal criteria  

estimated_probability
Estimated Probability (%) calculated according to MDS prodromal criteria  

prob_prodromalPD
Probable Prodromal PD calculated according to MDS prodromal criteria
